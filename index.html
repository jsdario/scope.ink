What is Scope?

 

Scope is a tool that enables you to measure developer impact on an organization. It prints visualizations based on how code contributions are performed, parametrizing cognitive values over raw metrics.

 

Track the developer impact

Boost your productivity

 

You are able to detect how good are the contributions to the code. Scope uses an own algorithm to measure different kinds of creative work: PRs, commits, reviews, tests, etc. so you can control better the time invested and be more efficient.


Motivate your team

Find the talent


It will be easier to find talented people in your company. With Scope, you can also motivate your team by giving them awards. Work better or you will pay the next beer!


Organize better your work

Encourage the use of good practices

 

With more control about the processes, the solutions are more easy to be implemented. In Scope, we are stubborn with the use of good practices within code crafting. We look to foment it and we are able to make you work efficiently by organizing much better your habits.

Simple and fast

We don't read the data, all the analysis is performed client-side there. Our mission is to create value an won't ever read and sell your data.
