What is Scope?

 

Scope is a tool that enables you to measure developer impact on an organization. It prints visualizations based on how code contributions are performed, parametrizing cognitive values over raw metrics.

 

Track the developer impact

 

You can measure the impact of a developer work in your organization. Analyze pull requests, commits and test reviews.

Motivate your team

Have a control about what's happening in your code. Reward your workers for the good work.

Increase your productivity

 

Detect the errors, find solutions and improve the quality of your work visualizating your own data.

Safe and sound

We don't read the data, all the analysis is performed client-side there. Our mission is to create value an won't ever read and sell your data.
